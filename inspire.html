Ruth Bader Ginsburg

"Women belong in all places where decisions are being made." -Ruth Bader Ginsburg

Ruth Bader Ginsburg was a Justice on the supreme court, who was known as a "feminist icon". RBG was the second women to be appointed to the supreme court and during her life time, she would fight for equality.

RBG inspires me everyday, because she knew what changes she wanted to make and fought hard to make sure that women were treated fairly in society.
