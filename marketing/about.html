About Us

The Longmont Humane Society is a non-profit organization, dedicated to helping animals find great homes.

This animal shelter is a temporary home to thousands of animals every year. This animal shelter serves:

  • Longmont
  • Frederick
  • Firestone
  • Lyons
  • Dacono
  • Mead
  • Some of Boulder County
